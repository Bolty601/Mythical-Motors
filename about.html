About Mythical Motors

Mythical Motors is a website created to showcase various vehicles from movies, tv shows and video games. The site focuses on a few that I personally find interesting. As someone who has a great appreciation for vehicles, especially in video games, I wanted to gather some of the fictional vehicles that stick out most to me, do a little background research on them (if possible), and showcase them all in one place.
